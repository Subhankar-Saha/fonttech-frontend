Franchise Overview

Overview :

Smart Retail Shop is an extension of Fonttech's ongoing efforts in creating more engagement points to improve consumer experience. Fonttech Smart Shops serve as an exclusive and franchised one-stop shops offering an opportunity to the consumer to experience and choose from a wide range of products under a single roof while providing an opportunity to our franchise partners to associate with us in our efforts of business expansion.

These exclusive shops also double as a platform for us to engage with consumers, understand their expectations and get valuable feedback which enables us to improve our product offerings, service and overall consumer experience

Our Offerings :

  • Smart Tv : Android LED Tv, Google LED Tv, WebOS LED Tv
  • Home Theater : Atoms, Bar, Tower, Trolly
  • Fans : Ceiling Fan, Wall Fan, Stand Fan
