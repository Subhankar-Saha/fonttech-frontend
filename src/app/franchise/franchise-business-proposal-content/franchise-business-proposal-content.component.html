Business Proposal

Initial Checklist

Franchise would be required to provide 150 - 200 sq. ft. carpet area in strong retail area on ground floor with frontage of 5 - 8 ft.

Warehouse required more than 300 sq. ft. carpet area.

Storage space for stock would be the onus of the franchise and additional expense, if any, for the same would be borne by the franchise.

Premium Electronics Store will be personally managed by the Franchise. Fonttech will hire a store manager for the managing the store. Franchise would also provide one dedicated resource in form of helper for upkeep and maintain hygiene factor of the store.

Fonttech Franchise Information

Costs Covered by Fonttech

  • Brand Advertisement: Managed by Fonttech.
  • Marketing Activity: Managed by Fonttech.
  • Electricity Bill: Managed by Fonttech.
  • Staff Hiring & Training: Managed by Fonttech.
  • Interior Fixtures and Branding Costs: Borne solely by Fonttech and will remain Fonttech’s property.

Store Setup and Maintenance

Store interiors, layout, and branding are done as per the guidelines laid down by Fonttech through its appointed designing agency. The Franchise is expected to maintain the high quality of the interior throughout the operation of the Smart Shop.

Maintenance Responsibility: Any subsequent maintenance cost, handling of damages, and wear-and-tear will be borne by the Franchise to conform to the quality standards as defined by Fonttech.

Benefits

  • No Royalty Fee: Enjoy zero royalty charges.
  • Zero Franchise Charge: No initial franchise fee required.
  • High Profit Potential: Earn up to 35% profit.
