Franchise Business Proposal

Initial Checklist

Franchise would be required to provide at least. 150 sq. ft. carpet area in strong retail area on ground floor with frontage of at least 5 ft. Warehouse required more than 300 sq. ft. carpet area.

Storage space for stock would be the onus of the franchise and additional expense, if any, for the same would be borne by the franchise.

Orient Electric Smart Shop will be personally managed by the Franchise. In absence of his personal presence, Franchise will hire a store manager for the managing the store. Franchise would also provide one dedicated resource in form of helper for upkeep and maintain hygiene factor of the store.

Investment Estimate

The initial set-up investment stands at an estimate of Rs. 20 Lakhs. The breakup is as follows:

Particulars Approx. Cost (in Indian Rupees)
Civil Work (False Ceiling, Floor Work, Glass Facade, Electric Fittings, Air Conditioner etc.) Rs. 5.5 Lakh
(varies with the store size, layout and variance in standardized rates)
Stationery and Office Equipment (Personal Computer, Licensed Tally Software, Television, CCTV, Printer, etc.) Rs. 2 Lakh
Display Stock Rs. 1.5 Lakh
(additional One Time Support of 50% off on MRP per SKU)
On Hand Saleable Stock Rs. 1.5 Lakh

Total Initial Investment (One-Time) Rs. 16-20 Lakh
