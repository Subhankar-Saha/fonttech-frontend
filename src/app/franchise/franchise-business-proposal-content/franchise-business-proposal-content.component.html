Initial Checklist

Franchise would be required to provide 150 - 200 sq. ft. carpet area in strong retail area on ground floor with frontage of 5 - 8 ft.

Warehouse required more than 300 sq. ft. carpet area.

Storage space for stock would be the onus of the franchise and additional expense, if any, for the same would be borne by the franchise.

Smart Retail Shop will be personally managed by the Franchise. In absence of his personal presence, Franchise will hire a store manager for the managing the store. Franchise would also provide one dedicated resource in form of helper for upkeep and maintain hygiene factor of the store.
